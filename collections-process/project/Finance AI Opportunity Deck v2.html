  • GPS last-seen, motion within 48h

Step 2C · Billing

Open invoices

Zoho · tagged via linked rider
  • Invoice 1–70 per rider
  • Status (Open / Paid / Cancelled)
  • Linked rider · linked bike IMEI
Existence failure
Invoice live, bike not deployed
Zombie invoice — cancel; rider corrected; cash, if any, routes to suspense.
Completeness failure
Bike deployed, no live invoice
Revenue leak — invoice raised same day, backdated by deployment date.
Orphan rider
Rider active, no bike linked
Pre-deployment or stale post-repossession status — corrected within 24h.
Orphan bike
Bike deployed, no rider
Onboarding gap — Ops + Collections jointly resolve; status corrected.
Why bike-first. The bike inventory in Wahu OS carries the immutable ownership tag (WF / TSA). Every rider in the master inherits the tag from their assigned bike, and every invoice inherits via the rider. Pulling the bike list first — then splitting — means the WF book and the TSA book never bleed into each other, no matter what happens to a rider downstream. Completeness target 100%, existence target 100%, both reported daily.

Three landing zones, one weekly close. (1) Rider Zoho — each withheld amount closes that rider's oldest overdue invoice, feeding straight into the Collections Report. (2) Wahu P&L — Wahu's commission booked as Fleet Revenue under BoltFood, with the cash leg in MTN Wallet. (3) TSA Payable — TSA's commission accrues each week and settles end of month.
