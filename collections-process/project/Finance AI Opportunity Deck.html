Collections Reconciliation —
process, data sources, and where Claude earns its keep.

Every weekday, three payment streams (MTN MoMo, Vodafone MoMo, Hero App) have to be matched against ~active rider invoices in Wahu OS and Zoho Billing, and reconciled by 12:00 GMT. This document walks the daily loop end-to-end, names the system of record behind every step, and flags the Claude skill that removes the manual work — without removing the four-eyes control.

Riders pay through three channels — the system has to listen to all of them.

Riders pay GHS 70 per day (or weekly lumps of 140 / 210 / 420) via MTN merchant 0598953614, Vodafone merchant 0200447867, or the Wahu Hero App. Each channel produces its own audit trail; only the Hero App is pre-tagged to a rider ID.

— no AI yet, this is just raw signal.

Three folders fill overnight; the recon job starts when they're stable at 09:00 GMT.

Inputs3 CSVs · ~hundreds of lines

09:00 — Pull and consolidate the three extracts into one workbook.

Collections Officer logs into both merchant portals, exports yesterday's transaction CSVs, and pulls the Hero App CSV from Wahu OS. Today these land in three different shapes (column orders, date formats, MSISDN encodings) and have to be reconciled into one workbook.

/MTN_Merchant/Exports/yyyy-mm-dd.csv /Voda_Merchant/Exports/yyyy-mm-dd.csv WahuOS > Reports > Hero Payments
Owner · Collections Officer · ~30 min today

CSV Ingest & Normalize

Claude reads all three CSVs, normalises columns (date, MSISDN, amount, ref, name) and emits a single canonical workbook. Same skill catches schema drift (a new column from a portal update) before it corrupts the day's recon.

Skill 01−25 min / day

09:30 — Auto-match: rider name + MSISDN, or MSISDN + standard amount.

Three rules clear the easy cases: exact name + registered MSISDN, registered MSISDN + amount ∈ {70, 140, 210, 420}, and Hero App pre-tagged lines (verify-only). Everything that auto-matches posts to the rider's ledger in Wahu OS.

WahuOS rider master WahuOS ledger Hero pre-tag
Owner · Wahu OS rules engine

Fuzzy Matcher

Claude tightens the auto-match band: tolerates name re-orderings ("Kofi A. Mensah" vs "Mensah Kofi"), spelling drift, and Akan/English variants. Lifts auto-match rate from ~70% to ~92%, shrinking the exception queue.

Skill 02+22pp auto-match

10:00 — Investigate the exception queue (today's most expensive step).

For each unmatched line: name missing or mis-spelled → look up sender MSISDN. Unknown MSISDN → call the sender from the portal number. Over-payment → carry credit. Under-payment → FIFO against oldest open invoice. Anything still unresolved by 48h drops into the MoMo Suspense GL.

WahuOS rider master MoMo Suspense GL Rider notes
Owner · Collections Officer · today: 60–90 min

Exception Triage Agent

Claude classifies every exception (name-drift / unknown sender / over-pay / under-pay / duplicate), proposes the most likely rider match with confidence, and drafts the verification SMS or call script. Officer approves with a click; ambiguous cases stay human.

Skill 03 + 04−45 min / day

11:00 — Post to Wahu OS ledger, then re-key each payment into Zoho Billing (FIFO).

The single largest operational risk in the workflow today: every matched payment is keyed manually into Zoho against the oldest open invoice, referencing the source transaction ID. Reversals are restricted to a Finance Officer (segregation of duties).

WahuOS ledger (post) Zoho Billing (manual key-in) Source TXN ID required
Owner · Collections Officer · today: 45–60 min

Zoho Bridge (until API)

Claude generates the per-rider posting set with source TXN IDs already attached, applies FIFO, and writes via Zoho Billing's API — or, in a fallback mode, drives a browser session against the Zoho UI. Eliminates re-keying entirely; medium-term retires the manual step.

Skill 05−55 min / day

12:00 — Four-eyes close. Variance investigated before recon is signed off.

Finance Officer verifies total value keyed into Zoho equals total matched in the recon workbook. Any variance is chased before sign-off. Workbook is signed by both officers and archived for 7 years.

Daily Recon Workbook Audit archive (7yr)
Owner · Finance Officer · control retained

Variance Detective

Claude runs the tie-out, surfaces the specific lines causing variance, and proposes the cause (duplicate post, wrong invoice number, missing TXN ID). Human still signs; the time-to-find-the-error collapses from minutes to seconds.

Skill 06−15 min / day

14:00 — Daily Reconciliation Report distributed to Head of Collections, Finance, Ops.

Eight sections: cash summary, matched vs unmatched, rider-level detail, risk flags raised, exceptions, weekly forecast, fleet utilisation, and lost-revenue calc (inactive bikes × ₵70/day).

Daily Recon Report WahuOS fleet + GPS Risk flags (auto)
Owner · Collections Officer · today: ~45 min

Report Drafter

Claude assembles all eight sections from the closed workbook, fleet feed and flags feed. Drafts the executive summary line ("collection rate 96.2% vs target 95%; 4 riders crossed into immobilisation queue") and ships PDF + Slack-friendly summary.

Skill 07−40 min / day

When riders fall behind — the graduated ladder

Every step logged in Wahu OS with timestamp, actor and evidence. Enforcement is defensible by design.
1–2days past due
ReminderAutomated nudge
Automated SMS + email reminder from Wahu OS. No human contact yet. Logged against rider record.
WahuOS notifications
Reminder Composer Claude personalises copy by rider's payment history & preferred channel — not just a template blast.
3–6days past due
Soft CollectionOfficer calls
Collections Officer calls the rider, negotiates a Promise-to-Pay (PTP) with date, logs outcome in Wahu OS. Broken PTPs auto-escalate the flag.
WahuOS > Rider > Contact log WahuOS > PTP register
Call Prep Brief + PTP Tracker Claude prepares a 5-line rider brief before the call (history, last excuse, GPS, score trend) and watches PTP fulfilment in the background.
7days past due
ImmobilisationHead of Collections signs
Operations issues remote immobilisation command via Wahu OS within 24h. GPS confirmed before/after. SMS + call explains what payment unlocks the bike. Reversal within 2h of cleared arrears.
WahuOS > Bike command GPS pre/post check
Immobilisation Pre-flight Claude assembles the sign-off pack: rider statement, contact attempts, score, GPS — so the Head of Collections approves in one screen.
8–14days past due
Hard CollectionField visit + demand letter
Daily calls. One field visit by the assigned agency (Horta or TSA). Formal demand letter issued. Bike stays immobilised until arrears clear OR a signed payment plan exists.
Agency client portal Demand letter template
Demand Letter Drafter Claude fills the demand letter from rider + statement + enforcement log. Officer reviews, signs, sends.
15–29days past due
Final NoticeGuarantor notified
Registered final demand. Guarantor notified. Repossession team briefed; asset located via GPS. Last 48h window for rider rehabilitation.
WahuOS > Guarantor record Field plan
Recovery Plan Synthesiser Claude proposes the field plan from GPS + neighbourhood + agency: route, safety notes, fallback windows.
30days past due
RepossessionHoC + Finance sign
Field Recovery recovers the bike within 24h of authorisation. Asset returned to fleet yard same day. Condition report signed by recovery + fleet manager. Account moves to Recovery status.
Agency recovery report WahuOS > Asset condition
Condition Report Capture Claude turns the fleet manager's photos + voice notes into a structured condition report against the bike's deployment baseline.
90+PAR 90 / write-off
Legal / Write-offHoC + CFO + CEO
Case file prepared (contract, statement, enforcement log, demand evidence, guarantor). External counsel issues letter of demand + court filing. On assignment to debt collector, balance is written off; recoveries booked as recovery on written-off debt.
Legal case file Write-off register
Case File Builder Claude compiles the legal pack end-to-end: every doc, every timestamp, every approver — externally-counsel-ready.

VODAFONE MERCHANT
0200447867

  • /Exports/Voda_{yyyy-mm-dd}.csv

HERO APP
Pre-tagged payments

Rider pays inside the app; the resulting transaction is already linked to the rider ID — no reconciliation needed, only verification.

  • WahuOS > /Reports/Hero_{date}.csv
  • cols: rider_id, amount, ref, ts
  • KPI: % share of all rider cash
